Why Next Play?

The name comes from that moment after a chunked wedge or lipped-out putt – you take a breath, reset, and focus on the next shot. Our gear is built for that reset.

Born on muni fairways, not in a boardroom.

Next Play started as a note in the back of a scorecard after another round with a crusty towel and a bent plastic repair tool. We didn’t want more clutter in the bag – we wanted a few pieces that felt good to use every time we reached for them.

Every item in the collection had to clear three checks: does it feel solid in the hand, does it look clean in our yellow / green / white colours, and does it earn its space on the bag or in the pocket.

We’re a small brand, which means short runs, tight quality control and designs that can actually change when we get feedback from people who play more golf than we do.
